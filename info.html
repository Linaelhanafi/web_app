African Youth Center for Sustainable Development

Creating opportunities for youth

Impact Assessment and Reporting: the african youth centre for SDGs tracks the progress and impact of the ventures supported. Regular impact assessments are conducted to measure the social, economic, and environmental outcomes achieved by each venture. This data is then utilized to create comprehensive reports highlighting the collective contribution towards the SDGs.

Investment and Funding Support: the african youth centre for SDGs acts as a bridge between impact investors, donors, and sustainable ventures. It actively seeks funding opportunities, connects ventures with potential investors, and assists in securing financial support for scaling up their operations.

Awareness and Advocacy: the african youth centre for SDGs engaged in advocacy efforts to raise awareness about the SDGs and the importance of sustainable development. It collaborates with media outlets, organizes public events, and conducts awareness campaigns to promote the SDGs and the impact of the supported ventures.

Capacity Building and Training: The centre provides capacity-building programs and training sessions to enhance the skills and knowledge of the participating ventures. This includes workshops on business development, sustainable practices, impact measurement, and financial management.
