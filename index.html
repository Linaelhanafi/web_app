African Youth Center for Sustainable Development

A Moroccan center that promotes integrated approaches to the implementation of the sustainable development goals, through leadership and capacity-strengthening of youth and the creation of development project ideas based on global cooperation.

Know more

It is a non-governmental organization that aims to contribute to the empowerment of youth and women in Morocco, with the aim of promoting civil, economic, social and cultural rights through comprehensive community projects.

  • The activities of the association do not have a political character. It exercises its activities freely; its positions are based on human values, tolerance, acceptance and openness to the outside world
  • African Youth Center for SDG’S is a gathering of youth full of passion, motivation and eager to learn and share under the umbrella of the Agenda 2063
  • A development-inspired youth center that works together to put ambitions into action.
